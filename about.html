About Us

bold. confidence. beyond.

At JLX Gemstones and Consultant, we are experts in sourcing and supplying exquisite gemstones, offering a carefully curated collection tailored for discerning jewelers, designers, and collectors. We believe in the unique narrative each stone carries and are dedicated to delivering gemstones that not only dazzle but also endure the test of time. Our stones are meticulously selected from prestigious sources worldwide, guaranteeing the highest quality and unmatched beauty.

Our extensive selection caters to various design needs, be it crafting an engagement ring, a striking necklace, or a one-of-a-kind piece of bespoke jewelry. With our presence in Asia, the USA, Australia, UAE, France, and Germany, we ensure a global reach to provide an array of precious and semi-precious stones suitable for any style and occasion.

Backed by a team of skilled gemologists and miners, our commitment to outstanding customer service ensures your journey to finding the perfect stone for your creations is seamless. With decades of experience in buying, selling, and supplying gemstones since 1985, we invite you to join us in embracing the art of fine jewelry with stones that exude elegance, sophistication, and artistry.
